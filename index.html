Welcome to Ozamiz

Ozamiz City, nestled in Misamis Occidental in northern Mindanao, Philippines, is a vibrant and scenic city known as the "Gem of Panguil Bay." It is a harmonious blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and modern development. The city is surrounded by picturesque landscapes, including lush mountain ranges, serene coastal areas, and the tranquil waters of Panguil Bay, making it a haven for nature lovers and adventurers.

Rich in history and culture, Ozamiz is home to iconic landmarks like Fort Santiago (Cotta Fort) and the Birhen sa Cotta Shrine, which reflect its Spanish colonial past and deep spiritual roots. The city celebrates its unique identity through colorful festivals such as the Subayan Keg Subanon Festival, showcasing the traditions of the Subanon tribe. As a hub for trade, education, and governance in the region, Ozamiz combines progress with a warm, small-town charm, offering a welcoming atmosphere for residents and visitors alike.

Mission

To build a safe, peaceful, and progressive city that provides equal opportunities for all its residents to grow and thrive. Ozamiz City is committed to promoting good governance, fostering inclusive development, and improving the overall quality of life for its people. We aim to protect and preserve our natural resources, celebrate our cultural heritage, and create an environment where every citizen feels valued, supported, and empowered. Through collaboration and innovative solutions, we seek to address challenges and achieve sustainable growth for the benefit of current and future generations.

Vision

Ozamiz City aspires to be a vibrant, united, and prosperous community where people live in harmony with each other and with nature. Our vision is to create a city that blends progress with tradition, where development is balanced with environmental preservation and cultural pride. We envision Ozamiz as a center of excellence in governance, education, and commerce, where everyone has the opportunity to contribute to and benefit from the city's success. A place of peace and resilience, Ozamiz will be a shining example of a city that values its heritage while embracing innovation for a sustainable and inclusive future.

Core Values

  • Honesty - We commit to transparency, integrity, and fairness in all our actions to build trust and confidence among our citizens.
  • Equality - We strive to treat everyone with respect and dignity, ensuring that all residents, regardless of background, have access to opportunities for growth and success.
  • Care for Nature - We take responsibility for protecting our environment, promoting sustainable practices, and preserving natural resources to safeguard the future of our community.
  • Cultural Pride - We celebrate the rich traditions, history, and diversity that make Ozamiz unique, fostering a deep sense of belonging and identity among our people.
  • Teamwork - We believe in the power of collaboration, valuing the contributions of individuals, communities, and institutions to achieve shared goals.
